About Us

Imminent is a one stop shop for specialized Computer Aided Design (CAD), Drafting, Cad Customization and business solutions. We serve global companies ranging from a mid sized business to multi disciplinary business corporations. Whether you need 2D drafting, 3D modeling , or design, we can expand your team, meet your deadlines, lower your costs, and impress your clients on your behalf.

As a valued business partner in the Engineering Design and Drafting industry for more than 2 + years, we understand the industry and its requirements. Each of our operations team members has an engineering background with relevant experience and expertise in their respective domains. Apart from that, we also have regular training and development programs to keep our team abreast of the latest industry developments, to stay at the cutting edge of technology and industry knowledge.
